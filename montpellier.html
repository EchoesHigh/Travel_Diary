Montpellier, France

Nuzzled away deep in the south of France, just outside the region of Provence and only 10 km from the Mediterranean Sea, Montpellier is undeniably one of the most charming cities in Europe. And because it’s such a compact and walkable city, you can enjoy the best of Montpellier in just a day or two. Montpellier oozes with that old world charm that characterises the provincial towns and villages in this idyllic part of the world, that charm that makes you want to sell everything you own and move to France. The city’s roots date back to the 10th century and the historic ‘Écusson’ (old town) is a labyrinth of well-worn sandstone buildings, of sun-bleached-mint window shutters and leafy little nooks filled with pretty café terraces. In many ways Montpellier offers all the joys of Paris – the art, the food and wine, the architecture and light – but in a much more smaller, more maanageageable and affordable package.
